Who am I?

My name is Isac, i am a student at Malmö University studying Interaction Design. I love designing experiences that feel natural and solve problems. I also do street Photagraphy and paint with Acrylics drawing insperation from real life and Budhism.

I work with Figma for prototyping, and I'm building my skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript to bring my design to life in a digital format. I also use Finalcut,Pixelmator Pro and Logic to edit and gain control over my creative work.
