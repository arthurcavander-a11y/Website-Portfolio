AirTag Case

A custom-designed case for the Apple AirTag built to attach to a variety of surfaces- blending everyday utility with considered physical design.

The Problem

The Apple Airtag is a powerful tracking tool but due to it's round shape it makes it difficult to attach discreetly to many everyday surfaces. I found that existing cases are sometimes to generic and don't consider the varity of contexts people actually use them in.

The process

I resarched how people and myself use the airtag in day to day life. I then sketched a few different ideas and modelled the ones i was happy with. I wanted the case to be as small as possible to not make it clunky and also be able to hang or be put in a bag without damage to the tag.

The Outcome

A slim, versatile AirTag case that can be attached to a wide range of surfaces without screws or adhesive. The design keeps the AirTag hidden and secure while reamining easy to remove when needed.
