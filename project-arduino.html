Arduino Nano Creature

A touch touch-sensitive creature that reacts to physical contact with vibration and sound-exploring how everyday objects can feel alive through interaction design

The Problem

Most physical objects we interact with daily gives no feedback. The challenge was to explore how a simple object could feel responsive, alive and emotionally engaging through touch

The process

I started by sketching different creature concepts and thinking about what kind of reactions would feel natural. I then prototyped with the Arduino Nano , connecting a touch sensor to trigger both a vibration motor and a small speaker. I played around with different vibrations and sounds to make it feel alive.

The Outcome

A small physical creature that vibrates and makes sound when you touch it's antenna. The project showed me by using simple electrionics you can create something that feels alive.
